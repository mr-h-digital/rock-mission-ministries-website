About Rock Mission

Built On The Rock

We are a non-profit Christian community outreach ministry based in Eersterivier, Cape Town, South Africa. Our mission is to bring the Gospel and practical support to the Cape Flats through consistent service, discipleship, and compassionate outreach.

Our Calling

Rock Mission Ministries serves communities impacted by poverty, trauma, addiction, and family breakdown. We believe faith in Jesus Christ transforms lives and restores dignity, hope, and purpose.

Our approach combines spiritual care with practical action, including youth development, community initiatives, and relational support that strengthens families and neighborhoods.
