Who We Are

Built On The Rock.

We are a non-profit Christian community outreach ministry based in Eersterivier, Cape Town, South Africa. Our goal is to reach the lost and broken for transformation in Christ Jesus.

We believe that faith in Christ Jesus — and the practical application of God's Word through the power of the Holy Spirit — has the power to change lives for the better, permanently.

In the Cape Flats, many face the crushing weight of poverty, trauma, broken homes, addiction, and gang violence. We don't look away. We show up — with the Gospel, with practical help, and with the unwavering message that your life matters to God.

Flagship Initiative

Community Hope Project

Our core outreach initiative that brings practical help — food parcels, counselling referrals, and prayer — directly into the homes and streets of the Cape Flats. We go where the need is greatest, led by love and the power of Christ.

🧒
Youth

Youth Empowerment

Mentorship, music, life skills and the Word — equipping the next generation to break cycles of poverty and violence through a transforming relationship with Christ.

💊
Recovery

Addiction Recovery Support

Walking alongside those battling addiction — providing spiritual support, practical care, and connection to rehabilitation resources in the greater Cape Town area.

🏠
Family

Family Restoration

Helping broken families find healing — through counselling support, prayer, and the transformative message that no family is beyond the reach of God's grace.

Rock Mission Ministries is a registered Non-Profit Organisation in South Africa. Donations may qualify for Section 18A tax deductibility.
